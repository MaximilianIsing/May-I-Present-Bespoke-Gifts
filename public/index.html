My Story

The Gift-Giver Who Couldn't Stop

How a lifetime of perfectly chosen presents turned into a calling.

I always had more free time than my friends — and somehow, I always found myself spending it searching for the perfect gift. Not a good gift. The gift. The one that makes someone stop mid-unwrap and look at you like you really, truly see them.

Every birthday, every anniversary, every random Tuesday when someone needed to know they were loved — I was there with something that felt impossibly right. Friends started calling me before any occasion. "Michele, what do I get her?" "Michele, his birthday is in three days."

So I turned it into a real service. Now I get to spend my days doing what I love most: listening, learning about people, and finding that one thing that makes them feel truly, deeply seen.

"I always believed that the right gift can say everything your words can't. That's what I do — I find the words in the form of something you can hold."

— Michele
